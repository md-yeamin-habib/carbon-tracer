Carbon Tracer is an interactive web application designed to analyze the environmental impact of specific geographic locations. By allowing users to select a location through an integrated map interface, the system gathers and processes relevant data to evaluate carbon emissions and overall ecological conditions. The goal is to provide users with a clear, accessible understanding of how different areas contribute to environmental change.

Once a location is selected, the application generates a structured report that includes an environmental analysis, practical recommendations, and projected future scenarios. These insights are intended to highlight key factors influencing emissions—such as urban density, transportation patterns, and nearby industrial activity—while also suggesting actionable steps to improve sustainability and reduce environmental harm.

In addition to current analysis, Carbon Tracer visualizes possible future outcomes based on different environmental approaches. By comparing positive and negative scenarios, users can better understand the long-term impact of decisions made today. This makes the application a useful tool for awareness, planning, and encouraging more sustainable practices at both individual and community levels.
